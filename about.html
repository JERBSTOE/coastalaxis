About Jared

The person behind the agency.

Meet Your Agent

I was born and raised on California's Central Coast and graduated from California Polytechnic State University (Cal Poly) in 2010 with a degree in Finance.

I started my insurance career with AAA, where I learned the foundation of personal insurance and client service. After managing offices along the Central Coast and later specializing in commercial insurance at a large independent agency, I saw firsthand how important it is to structure coverage correctly — not just sell a policy.

In 2018, I opened Coastal Axis Insurance to bring that combined experience in both personal and commercial insurance together under one roof. My goal has always been simple: help clients truly understand their coverage and make confident, informed decisions.

Outside of work, I spend most of my time with my wife, our baby girl, and our golden retriever. I enjoy hiking, beach volleyball, hockey, golf, and surfing — and I'm grateful to live and work in a place that allows me to do all of it.
